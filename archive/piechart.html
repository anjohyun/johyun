Minecraft Survey Usability Result
Players mentioned that the UI design made what was going on pretty clear. This overall high rating result implies the instructions in each escene page were helpful in introducing mechanics.
58.8% of participants of Minecraft said the controller sometimes (once or twice) messed up against their purpose. 29.4% said never, 6% of participants said the controller had messed up more than twice.
Graphics and Art style was rated fairly high throughout the participants. 11.8% of participants Strongly agreed that Minecraft has visually attractive elements and polished graphics. 52% of participants agreed, 17.6% neutral, and 15% disagreed
The Overall Game quality with Environment art was rated evenly. This implies that the UI functions very well as a gameplay narrative and world introduction
The one issue was that about half of participants expressed the issue of lacking specific goals or directions for the Minecraft beacuse they felt like playing Legos on the comuter. The other half were satisfied with the fact that Minecraft is mostly about the players coming up with their own creativity. This result suggests that some players wish to have more clues to experience and stimulation to continue the game.
